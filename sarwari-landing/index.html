The Problem

Millions commute blind every day

Kathmandu Valley has 4+ million daily commuters with no reliable digital tools for public transport.

No Route Information

Google Maps shows driving paths, not actual bus routes. Nepal's informal transit is invisible online.

Expensive Alternatives

Ride-booking apps cost 5-10x the bus fare. Not everyone can afford them daily.

Ask a Stranger

The most common method — unreliable, time-consuming, and impossible for tourists.

No Live Tracking

You stand at a stop with zero visibility. Is the bus 2 minutes away or 30?

The Solution

One platform that answers every question — from "Which bus?" to "How much?"
